# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is MA Root Observer?

MA Root Observer is a model-agnostic governance framework that provides independent assessment, recommendations, and transparent logging for AI systems.

---

## Is MA Root Observer an AI model?

No.

It is an observer framework that can operate alongside AI models, agents, workflows, and decision systems.

---

## Does the Observer control the Entity?

No.

The Observer provides guidance and assessments.

Decision authority remains with the Entity or the governing organization.

---

## Can the Observer block actions?

Not by itself.

Organizations may create policies that respond to Observer assessments.

Policy enforcement is separate from the Observer.

---

## Why not build governance directly into the model?

Embedded governance changes when models change.

MA Root Observer provides a consistent governance layer independent of the underlying model.

---

## Does this framework support only LLMs?

No.

Potential use cases include:

* AI assistants
* AI agents
* Workflow engines
* Business process systems
* Human review systems

---

## Is the framework tied to a specific vendor?

No.

The framework is designed to be model agnostic.

---

## Why are observations logged?

Logging improves:

* Transparency
* Accountability
* Auditability
* Historical learning

---

## Will logs grow indefinitely?

Not necessarily.

Implementations may use:

* Tiered storage
* Consolidation
* Summarization
* Pattern-based retention

---

## Does the Observer make decisions?

No.

The Observer assesses and recommends.

The Entity decides.

---

## What problem is MA Root Observer trying to solve?

The framework seeks to provide independent assessment and transparent oversight without removing autonomy from the systems it observes.

---

## Does every Entity need a dedicated Observer?

No. MA Root Observer defines a logical observer relationship, not a required deployment topology. Implementations may use dedicated observers, shared observer pools, or hybrid models. Observation Tier represents evaluation depth.Assessment Outcome represents the Observer's conclusion.e, not the number of Observer instances assigned to an Entity.
